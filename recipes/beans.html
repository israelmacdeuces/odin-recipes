BEANS RECIPE

The making of beans

Beans is one of ghana cheap foods which they love enjoying. myself included

it is made up beans and ripe plantain eaten with red oil

Ingredients

  • beans
  • ripe plantain
  • salt
  • oil

STEPS

  1. boil water
  2. add beans and salt to taste
  3. boil oil
  4. and plantain till cooked
