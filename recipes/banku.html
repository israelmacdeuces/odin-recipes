BANKU AND TILAPIA

How to make banku and tilapia

banku and tilapia is a ghanian delicacy with consist of corn doe and fish

It is eaten with grinded pepper and shito, with any other combination of your choice

INGREDIENTS

  • corn doe
  • tomatoes
  • onion
  • salt
  • green bell pepper
  • tilapia

STEPS

  1. boil water
  2. add corn doe
  3. stir till hard paste texture acquired and shape into balls
  4. mix tomatoes pepper and onion together and grind till desired texture
