Lasagna

THE MAKING OF LASAGNA

Lasagna is a type of pasta, possibly one of the oldest types, made of very wide, flat sheets.

Either term can also refer to an italian dish made of stacked layers of lasagna.

INGREDIENTS

  • noodles
  • sauce
  • cheese
  • meat
  • sausage

STEPS

  1. boil pasta
  2. prepare meat
  3. combine meat mixture/li>
  4. layer and bake
